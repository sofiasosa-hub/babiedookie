SOFIA SOSA

Hello, my name is Sofia Sosa. I am a Miami-based artist currently pursuing a BFA in graphic design. My art focuses mainly on line work, using it to evoke powerful emotion and communicate textures. While I work, I create lines and shapes as intuitively as possible, focusing more on the specific characteristics of the emotions I want to convey and how those translate into visual shapes. With a strong visual influence from the natural world, a big basis of my work is based off of my own feelings of change and evolution. I enjoy combining both digital and physical elements in my works to build up layers and visual complexity.
